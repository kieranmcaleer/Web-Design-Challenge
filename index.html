Summary: Latitude vs. X

This website is made to compare the latitude of different cities to different variables. These variables include the Max Temperature, Humidity, Cloudiness, and Wind Speed. The data is taken from the OpenWeatherMap API. The graohs were assembled in MatPlotLib.
